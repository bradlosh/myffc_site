You will be enriched in every way so that you can be generous on every occasion, and through us your generosity will result in thanksgiving to God. 2 Corinthians 9:11
We believe that God loves a cheerful giver and at the heart of the Christian faith is generosity. We know our God is a generous giver and want to be more like Him. The Word of God calls us to be stewards of what we're given and to follow the principles of tithes and offerings as a way to establish His church and reach others with the gospel. We're never more like our God when we give, so we never hesitate to be generous.
